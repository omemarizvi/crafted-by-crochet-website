Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to make a custom order?

Custom orders typically take 3-7 days depending on complexity. We'll provide an estimated timeline when you contact us.

What are your shipping times?

Orders within Karachi are shipped within a week depending on the complexity. For orders outside of Karachi, please reach out to us to discuss shipping options and timelines.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers only. Payment details will be provided after order confirmation.

Do you offer custom designs?

Absolutely! We love creating unique pieces tailored to your preferences. Contact us with your ideas and we'll work together to bring your vision to life.

What materials do you use?

We use high-quality yarns and materials to ensure durability and beauty. All materials are carefully selected for their softness, color vibrancy, and longevity.

How do I care for my crochet items?

Most items can be gently hand-washed in cool water and laid flat to dry. Specific care instructions are provided with each order to help your items last for years to come.
